Nathan Ubajaka — Module 1 Progress Report

Tutor: Marshall Jacob
Program: MathCloud Tutorial
Duration: 4 Weeks (8 Sessions | 16 Hours)

Week 1

I began by observing Nathan’s strengths and weaknesses in spelling, reading, and comprehension. His willingness to learn and adapt quickly to a new teacher was commendable. However, his reading fluency, spelling accuracy, and comprehension skills require improvement to reach international standards.

Week 2

I introduced digital learning tools, particularly a spelling enhancement application. With consistent use, Nathan’s spelling skills improved from 35% to 52%, showing clear progress and growing confidence.

Week 3

Using an interactive reading and grammar platform, Nathan’s comprehension skills gradually improved. He now confidently identifies nouns, adjectives, verbs, and adverbs within simple sentences. His engagement and focus have notably increased.

Week 4

This week focused on reinforcing spelling, comprehension, and grammar through practical exercises and contextual reading. Nathan practiced sentence formation, short paragraph reading, and discussed short stories to enhance inferential understanding and vocabulary. In grammar, he applied parts of speech to form accurate and meaningful sentences, strengthening his communication and writing clarity. His confidence and enthusiasm for learning continue to grow impressively.

Resources and Tools Used

  • Google Classroom
  • Spelling Enhancement Application
  • Comprehension Web Application
  • Academic Portal
  • Amira Tutor (pending activation)

Recommendation

I recommend establishing an instant follow-up and communication channel to enable more frequent feedback and updates on Nathan’s progress between sessions.

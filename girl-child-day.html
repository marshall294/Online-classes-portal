About & Vision

The She Reigns Empowerment & Support Initiative, in collaboration with Matori Foundation, is committed to creating lasting change by:

Our Mission

To educate, empower, and uplift young girls through hygiene awareness, self-esteem coaching, leadership training, and mentorship — so they can become confident, healthy, and impactful leaders.

Our Vision

A Nigeria where every girl enjoys her rights, leads boldly, and thrives in safety, health, and opportunity.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the date, time, and location of the event?

The event is on 11th October 2025, starting at 10:00 AM. Location: BMUKO Community, Dutsen Alhaji, Abuja.

Who can attend?

The program is open to girls, young women, community leaders, volunteers, and supporters who care about girls’ welfare.

How can I volunteer?

You can send a message to shereignsempowerment@gmail.com or call 0816 215 725. We welcome help with facilitation, logistics, fundraising, mentorship, and more.

How are donations used?

Donations support hygiene kits, training materials, volunteer coordination, transport, venue setup, and outreach to underserved communities.
